a wedding in India

my friend Aashna invited me to her beautiful wedding in India!

I was so grateful to be given this opportunity, and I soaked in every moment.

step one: preparations before departure

I rented a lehenga and played dress up in all the sparkly dresses I could find

step two: travel to Udaipur

we flew to Udaipur and had a layover in Delhi Airport

we saw rickshaws and cows on the road the second we drove out of the airport

step three: admire the scenery

my jaw was on the floor

step four: meet the girls

these girls took me under their wing and brought me along to all the ceremonies

they truly made my experience so much more memarable and authentic

I would have been entirely lost without them!

step five: turmeric ceremony!

started to feel like I had been dropped into a bollywood movie

let the dancing begin!

step six: explore the palace

we stayed at the palace for two nights and I was in awe of the colors and details everywhere I looked

step seven: mehendi ceremony

maybe my favorite part of the wedding

I was so impressed at the speed and skill of the ladies doing the art

there was also a fascinating traditional dance performance

step eight: wedding ceremony at the Gurdwara !

the bridesmaids have to steal the groom's shoes and hold them hostage

after this, Aashna and Devraj were officially married

step nine: preparations for the big celebration

I was so excited to put on my lehenga

step ten: the celebration

we celebrated by wearing our most beautiful gowns, performing and dancing together!

I had such a wonderful time, I will cherish these memories forever!

by emma andrea
