my favourite cafe in district 1

Little Hanoi Egg Coffee Cafe (Ky Con)

This cute cafe has become a staple for me. I love to come here to study because of the cozy atmosphere and the kind staff.

And, of course, because the eggs benedict is the best I've ever had, and the egg coffee is mind-blowing.

I've brought my boyfriend and my new friends from school, and everyone loved it!
